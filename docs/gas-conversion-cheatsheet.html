Pitfalls

Keep the Basis Visible

  • IEA `TJ gross` is HHV/GCV. Many plant efficiencies and some balances use LHV/NCV.
  • `bcm` is not a fixed energy unit across countries; gas quality changes energy per cubic metre.
  • Use the project factor for model consistency even when market sources round to `36 TBtu/bcm`, `0.9 Mtoe/bcm`, or `1.38 bcm/Mt LNG`.
  • Prefer cargo heat content over tonne-to-bcm shortcuts when pricing or reconciling customs data.
  • JODI `TOTDEMC` is apparent demand; storage injections and withdrawals can separate it from end-use demand.
