Calculated Demand i JODI calculated demand is production plus imports minus exports, adjusted for stock change. China stock change is reported as zero in this dataset, so this is apparent demand and can include gas going into storage.

Data mechanics

How the JODI View Works

  1. Read China `NATGAS` monthly rows from the JODI Gas World Database.
  2. Convert JODI `M3` rows from million cubic meters to bcm.
  3. Order the selected gas year from October through September.
  4. Display only reported JODI actual months.
  5. Check the balance residual: production + imports - exports - stock change - calculated demand.
