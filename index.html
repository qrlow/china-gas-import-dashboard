How the Forecast Works

  1. Start from JODI monthly China gas balance actuals.
  2. Split total demand into power, industrial, buildings/city gas, and transport using editable shares and seasonal profiles.
  3. Apply driver adjustments for weather, fuel switching, power output mix, industrial activity, policy, and prices.
  4. Forecast domestic production, storage change, exports, and pipeline route flows.
  5. Use contracted LNG first, then solve the remaining LNG gap as spot need.
